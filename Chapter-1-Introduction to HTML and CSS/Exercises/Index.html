Make Your Own Delicious Pizza at Home!

Discover the joy of making fresh, homemade pizza with our easy-to-follow recipe. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned cook, this recipe is designed to bring restaurant-quality pizza right to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into a dough.
  3. Cover and let rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Spread pizza sauce over the dough and add toppings.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
